# Code of Conduct

This project follows a minimal standard of respectful and constructive interaction.

## Expectations

- Be respectful and professional
- Focus on technical merit and correctness
- Avoid personal attacks or inflammatory language

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Disruptive or non-constructive contributions

## Enforcement

Issues and pull requests may be moderated to maintain a productive environment.

Participation in the project implies acceptance of these guidelines.
